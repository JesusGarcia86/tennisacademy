About Elite Performance Tennis Academy USA



Who we are

Elite Performance Tennis Academy USA is a high performance tennis academy that focus in player development like technical, tactical, physical and mental development.


What makes us different?

We believe in planning and organization to get the best out of the players, we have a periodization system that allows us to plan each training depending on what stage of the periodization we are, that way we can focus in a particular area of p[ayer development.


Our Goals

Our goals are to assist our players and their families to achieve their improvement goals and set up a path (short, middle and long term goals) to get closer to their dreams.


Our Vision

We believe in hard work and discipline, we believe that technique is the structure of a good tennis player complemented by speed, strength, mental toughness, smart decision making, etc.
